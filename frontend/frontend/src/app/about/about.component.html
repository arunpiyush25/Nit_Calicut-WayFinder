About NITC WayFinder

Your trusted navigation companion at NIT Calicut.

Our Vision

To provide the most seamless and intuitive navigation experience for students, staff, and visitors at NITC, ensuring they never lose their way and can explore the campus with confidence.

Our Mission

To integrate advanced mapping technologies, user-friendly design, and real-time data to deliver an innovative tool tailored to the unique needs of the NITC community.

Features

  • Interactive campus maps with points of interest.
  • Real-time weather updates for any location.
  • Search functionality for landmarks and hostels.
  • Responsive design for desktop and mobile users.
